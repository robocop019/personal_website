Introduction

When I went to college I had no sense of what I wanted to do in life. I ended up selecting business as my major when I started school and quickly discovered that a career in business was not something that I wanted or enjoyed. I eventually found what I did want to do: programming. I started by teaching myself HTML and CSS and then joined the coding bootcamp Actualize. There I learned more advanced topics, how to think as a developer, and practiced pair-programming. I am now a full-stack software developer with a passion for finding solutions to complex problems. Having worked on projects of my own and as a team of developers, I have experience in different development environments and am always looking for new challenges and opportunities.
